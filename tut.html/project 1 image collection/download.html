The 10 Best Button-Down Shirts Men Will Want to Wear Everywhere This Season

The sharp style you want meets the comfort you need.

By Adam Mansuroglu
Dec 30, 2020
Huckberry

If there’s one shirt that can be easily level-up any man’s style and flatter his bod, it’s the button down. Even though the versatile collared shirt is one major wardrobe essential that every guy should own, most are less-than-thrilled to add button-down shirts to their close—and we get it. When it comes to sharp style, dressing up is usually considered less comfortable than your go-to hoodie or T-shirt. But thanks to the stellar offerings from some of your favorite shirting brands, the button down is made more comfortable than ever with fabric innovations to help you move with ease throughout you busy day. Dress up these flattering shirts with tailored trousers, or dress them down with your favorite pair of distressed jeans, the best button-down shirts are total game changers for the ultimate style flex with timeless appeal.

What to Look for When Shopping for Button-Down Shirts

If you’re confused about the difference between button-down shirt and a button-up shirt, the terms have become interchangeable (and it’s not worth worrying about). The only real difference between the two shirts is that a button down refers to additional buttons that hold down your collar flaps—it’s that simple.

What you should consider when shopping for a great button-down shirt is the fit and fabric. While some men might feel comfortable with a relaxed-fit shirt, it’s best to opt for a slim-fit that flatters your torso, but won’t cling to your body. Even if you fell off your fitness game this past summer, a slim-fit shirt will hug your arms and broad shoulders, but won’t showcase any imperfections in your midsection.

Maybe your first time wearing a standard Oxford shirt wasn’t the most comfortable experience, but thanks to advances in fabric technology, there’s a wide variety of button-downs with great stretch and moisture-wicking capabilities for added comfort. Worried about looking sharp after an active day in your button down? Thankfully, some of the best shirts are also wrinkle-resistant, so you can look great from office hours to happy hour. Whether you’re looking for a shirt that’s lightweight and breathable, or looking for a heavyweight option to keep you warm when that fall chill hits, there are plenty of button-down options that are equally stylish and comfortable.

Now that you know what to look for, check out the 10 best button-down shirts that’ll make any guy look like a total boss.
